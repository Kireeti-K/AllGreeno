Account Deletion: Ensuring User Control and Data Privacy

Welcome to AllGreeno Account Information page. We are committed to providing our users with a seamless and transparent experience when it comes to managing their accounts and personal data. In compliance with Google Play Store's requirements, we have implemented an account deletion feature that empowers you to take control of your data.

Account Deletion Process

If you have created an account within AllGreeno, you have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data. We value your privacy and are here to assist you through the process. Here's how you can initiate the account deletion process:

From Within the App:

Open All Greeno on your device. Navigate to your account settings or profile section. Look for the "Account Deletion" option. Follow the on-screen instructions to request the deletion of your account.

Outside the App (Website):

Please email to info@allgreeno.com from your registered EmailId with subject Account Deletion, Our customer service will deal with your request as soon as possible.

Data Deletion and Privacy

When you request the deletion of your account, we will permanently remove all the data associated with your account from our systems. This includes your personal information, preferences, activity history, and any other data linked to your account. Please note that temporary deactivation, disabling, or freezing of accounts is not the same as account deletion, and we are committed to ensuring your data is completely removed upon your request.

Stay Informed

For a more detailed understanding of our account deletion policy and practices, we encourage you to review the Google Play Help Center article on account deletion as well as the article on updating your Data Safety form. These resources will provide you with valuable insights into how we handle account deletion requests and safeguard your data.

Thank you for choosing AllGreeno. We are dedicated to ensuring your data privacy and providing you with a secure and enjoyable experience.
